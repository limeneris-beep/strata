The Layers

Three sources of liquidity.
One unified book.

01
Highest Priority

Real Limit Orders

User limit orders escrowed in on-chain UserVault PDAs. Hard commitment — funds are locked. Settle immediately when matched. GTC, IOC, FOK, Post-Only. Green and red bars on the book. Price-time priority.

02
Medium Priority

Intent Orders

Trusted market makers post soft RFQ-based commitments visible in gold. When a taker hits that level, the matching engine sends a parallel RFQ to all MMs. First responder fills. Non-responders are penalized via on-chain reputation. MMs profit by quoting tighter than AVL — hedge cost ≈ 0.

03
Fallback — Guaranteed Fill

AVL — Aggregated Virtualized Liquidity

Jupiter/AMM mirrored depth displayed as translucent bars. The Depth Sampler continuously polls Jupiter to map available liquidity at every price level. When hit, a fresh quote is fetched and executed in a single atomic Solana transaction via CPI. No funds move until execution. The book is never empty.

01 — SAMPLE

Map the curve

Depth Sampler calls Jupiter across 12 size increments to map available AMM liquidity at every price level — 40-60 virtual levels per side.

02 — MATCH

Waterfall fill

Every taker order routes through priority. Real orders fill first, then intent RFQ, then AVL fallback — guaranteeing 100% fill, every time.

03 — SETTLE

Atomic on-chain

Single Solana transaction via Jupiter CPI. Price guard reverts if execution deviates beyond slippage tolerance. All or nothing.

Structural Edge

We don't pay market makers.
They quote anyway.

Traditional CLOBs spend $1M+/year per pair on MM rebates, taker subsidies, and token incentives. Strata doesn't need to. AMM depth is already on the book — market makers aren't building from scratch. They capture spread inside a pre-quoted curve. Hedge cost ≈ 0. Same AMMs, same chain.

Deep books without paid MM programs. Structural, not promotional.

Beyond Spot

Financial primitives built
on visible depth.

Visible depth isn't just a UX feature. It's an ingredient for entirely new financial products — ones that can only exist when liquidity is transparent and programmatic.

🎲

Liquidity Prediction Markets

"Will there be >500 SOL of depth at $150 in the next hour?" Bet on the book itself. Predictions feed back into depth — more bets create more informed liquidity. The book becomes self-aware.

💸

Yield-Bearing Limit Orders

Resting orders earn option premium while they wait. Locked capital works across protocols — the order stays available, the maker collects yield. Every limit order becomes a dual-purpose position.

⌛

Ultra-Short Options

Calls and puts with 30-min to 4-hour expiries displayed inline alongside spot orders. Spot and derivatives in one interface — not two products. If you can see the book, you can bet on where it's going.

📊

Concentrated Liquidity Ranges

Stake collateral into a price range with leverage — $1K represents $3-5K of depth. Ranges slot into the book alongside real orders. No bot, no RPC nodes, no impermanent loss. Just spread capture.
