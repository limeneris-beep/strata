LAYER 1
Highest Priority

Real Limit Orders

User orders escrowed in on-chain UserVault PDAs. Hard commitment — funds are locked. Settle immediately when matched. GTC, IOC, FOK, Post-Only. Green and red bars on the book.

LAYER 2
Medium Priority

Intent Orders

Trusted market makers post soft RFQ commitments visible in gold. Parallel RFQ to all MMs at match. First responder fills. Non-responders penalized via on-chain reputation. Hedge cost ≈ 0.

LAYER 3
Universal Fallback

AVL Depth

Jupiter/AMM mirrored liquidity displayed as translucent bars. Fresh quote + atomic CPI execution on match. Price guard on-chain. The book is never empty. No funds move until execution.

How It Works

Sample. Match. Settle.

Sample the Curve

Depth Sampler polls Jupiter across 12 size increments to map AMM liquidity at every price level — 40–60 virtual levels per side.

⇄
02

Waterfall Fill

Every taker order routes through priority: real orders first, then intent RFQ, then AVL fallback — 100% fill, every time.

Beyond Spot

Financial primitives
built on visible depth.

Visible depth isn't a UX feature. It's a substrate for entirely new financial products — ones that can only exist when liquidity is transparent and programmatic.

◈

Liquidity Prediction Markets

"Will there be >500 SOL at $150 in the next hour?" Bet on the book itself. Predictions feed back into depth — more bets create more informed liquidity.

◆

Yield-Bearing Orders

Resting limit orders earn option premium while they wait. Locked capital works across protocols. Every limit becomes a dual-purpose position.

◉

Ultra-Short Options

30-min to 4-hour calls and puts displayed inline alongside spot orders. Spot and derivatives in one interface — not two products.

◎

Concentrated Liquidity Ranges

Stake collateral into a range with leverage — $1K represents $3–5K of depth. Ranges slot into the book. No bot, no RPC nodes, no IL.
