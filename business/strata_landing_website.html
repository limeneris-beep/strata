Order book DEXes die when the book looks empty. Strata stacks liquidity in priority layers, so there's always depth to trade against — and every taker order is guaranteed to fill.

Real Orders Layer 1 · highest priority

On-chain limit orders escrowed in user vaults. Hard, locked commitments. Self-custodial — cancel and withdraw anytime, even if the matcher is down.

MM Intents Layer 2 · deep & capital-efficient

Aggregated Virtualized Liquidity renders every AMM on Solana as visible book depth. The book is never empty. Every remaining order fills here.

The core innovation

AVL

Aggregated Virtualized Liquidity takes the AMM liquidity already on Solana — Raydium, Orca, Meteora and beyond — and renders it as visible order book depth. Powered by Strata's in-house Sonar engine.

  • Every AMM becomes a silent market maker
  • No new pools, no fragmentation, no funds move until execution
  • On-chain price guard, atomic swap — guaranteed fill or full revert
◈
Capital-efficient depth

MM Intents

Market makers quote across dozens of price levels from a single funded vault — no capital locked per level. Backed by reputation and stake, not latency.

  • One deposit covers many levels of depth
  • Multi-MM pooling = deeper displayed size per level
  • Compete on spread and reliability, not speed races

Orbital Pools

The first production Orbital AMM — a new pool design where one n-asset pool serves every pair at once. Concentrated, capital-efficient liquidity for any basket of correlated assets.

  • One pool, every pair — triangular-arb-free by design
  • Concentrated into the band where assets trade for extreme capital efficiency
  • First basket live: liquid staking tokens, swappable deep and cheap
First on Solana — plausibly first anywhere

Orbital Pools.

Orbital generalizes concentrated liquidity to an n-dimensional sphere. Instead of one pair per pool, a single pool serves every pair in a basket simultaneously — deeper, cheaper, and arbitrage-free across the whole set.

The first basket is liquid staking tokens — up to eight LSTs in one pool. Maker-funded and backstopped by AVL from day one, so there's no dead cold start: the book is live and deep before the first maker arrives. The same engine replicates across stables, BTC wrappers, and any basket of tight-band correlated assets.
